Resume

Hi there! I’m a Computer Science Master’s student at NYU’s Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences.

Before NYU, I was a Deep Learning Engineer at Flixstock, where I worked on GANs for photorealistic image editing. Previously, I was a Research Engineer at IBM Research India where I developed novel techniques for conversational question answering with Danish Contractor and his team.

I got the opportunity to pursue my bachelor’s thesis as a Research Intern at Microsoft Research India where I worked with Navin Goyal on translating code to natural language.

I graduated from BITS Pilani with a B.E. (Hons) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. For more information, feel free to reach out to me using any of the social platforms.
